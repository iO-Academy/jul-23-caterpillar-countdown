Caterpillar Countdown

Instructions

Welcome to Caterpillar Countdown!

This is a game to test your mental agility at selecting numbers

The game consists of rounds and within each round you will see 3 to 5 different numbers between 0-100

You need to click on the numbers in ascending order to allow you to progress to the next round

Each round that you complete will add 1 point to your score

See how many rounds you can do in 30 seconds!

BEWARE: an incorrect number selected will reset the numbers picked so far in the round so you will have to reclick the numbers
